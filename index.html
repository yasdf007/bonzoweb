True open source community-driven developed Discord bot.

Bonzo is a bot built for community by community.

It is completely free to use and also open sourced.

Give it a try!

We use numerous popular technologies (most of which are also open source) such as:

Core:

  • Python 3.9
  • discord.py
  • discord-py-interactions

Music:

  • Lavalink
  • Wavelink

API:

  • OpenWeather
  • GoogleAPI
  • CoinMarketCapAPI (closed-source)
At the moment we're actively maintain this project on behalf ot its community and regarding to actual feedback we recieve via our hub - AloneClub.
Please keep in mind that Bonzo is non-commercial project, therefore we provide limited support and updates. This website is also a part of Bonzo ecosystem and will be updated in nearest future.
